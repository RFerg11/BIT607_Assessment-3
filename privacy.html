At Snazzy Paws Grooming, we respect your privacy and are committed to complying with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website or services.

1. What Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you:

  • Complete a booking or contact form
  • Contact us via phone or email

This information may include:

  • Your name
  • Contact details (email, phone number, address)
  • Appointment and pet information

We only collect information necessary to provide our grooming services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and manage bookings
  • Respond to enquiries
  • Communicate appointment details
  • Improve our services

We will not use your information for any purpose unrelated to providing our services without your consent.

3. Sharing Your Information

Your personal information is not sold or traded. We may only share your information if:

  • Required by law
  • Necessary for our service delivery (e.g., third-party booking systems, where applicable)

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your information against:

  • Loss
  • Unauthorised access
  • Misuse

Information is stored securely and accessed only by staff who need it to perform their duties.

5. Access and Correction Rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections if your information is inaccurate

To make a request, contact us using the details below. We will respond in accordance with the Privacy Act 2020.

6. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies for basic functionality and to improve user experience. Cookies do not collect personally identifiable information. You can disable cookies in your browser if you prefer.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
